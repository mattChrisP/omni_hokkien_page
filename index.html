    Staged training

    Capability,
    built layer by layer.

    The model is progressively specialised—from understanding Hokkien to carrying its response across architectures and speaking it aloud.

    1. 01
      Ground understanding

    3. 03
      Shape the voice

      Adapt the bridge and Talker together for fluent speech output.

    05 / Why Hokkien

    予話繼續講落去。

    For the people who
    still speak it—and
    those learning next.

    In Singapore, Hokkien remains part of everyday life for many seniors, but it is not always passed to the next generation. OmniHokkien explores how speech technology can support Hokkien-speaking seniors in daily interactions, help younger people learn the language, and keep an important part of local cultural heritage in active use.

    06 / Try it

    one conversation.

    Python · spoken question 
    from omnihokkien import OmniHokkien

model = OmniHokkien.from_pretrained(
    "iNLP-Lab/OmniHokkien",
    device_map="cuda:0",
)

output = model.generate(
    audio="question.wav",
    language="nan",
)

print(output.text)
